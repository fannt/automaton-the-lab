Workshop

Composing with sound spatialization

Sound diffusion across multiple speakers is an art form, especially where real-time control is concerned. This workshop features live sound spatialization techniques using Ambisonics and parametric-procedural diffusion methods.

Participants will bring to the workshop an existing sound piece that is suitable for diffusion across 4-8 channels.

At the end of the workshop, participants will setup their multichannel sound piece to be presented during the Automaton Lab exhibition in the unique sonic environment of the Kleiner Wasserspeicher.

Workshop participants requirements
Laptop with Reaper installed, if possible a sound card with more than 2 outputs, and a midi-controller.

Information

Location

The Lab takes place at the Kleiner Wasserspeicher. The entrance is at the northwest corner; you can find it on the map here.

Since it is a former water tank, the ambient temperature is around 12 degrees celsius; we advise that you bring a sweater!

Hours

The exhibition is open from Wednesday 22.06 until Saturday 25.06, from 12:00 until 20:00.

Tickets
