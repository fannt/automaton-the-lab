Workshop

Composing with sound spatialization

Sound diffusion across multiple speakers is an art form, especially where real-time control is concerned. This workshop features live sound spatialization techniques using Ambisonics and parametric-procedural diffusion methods.

Participants will bring to the workshop an existing sound piece that is suitable for diffusion across 4-8 channels.

At the end of the workshop, participants will setup their multichannel sound piece to be presented during the Automaton Lab exhibition.

Workshop participants requirements
Laptop with Reaper installed, if possible a sound card with more than 2 outputs, and a midi-controller.
This workshop takes place from 18:00 until 22:00 on Wednesday 22.06 and Thursday 23.06.
